🇦🇺AustraliaPacific

Australia delivers staggering scale — the Great Barrier Reef, Sydney's harbour icons, Outback red-earth horizons, and laid-back coastal cities — in a country roughly the size of the contiguous US. Long-haul but worth every hour: this is a true bucket-list, multi-week itinerary destination for American travelers.

⭐ Why Sell This Destination
  • Direct nonstop service from multiple US gateways — no more brutal multi-stop routings
  • English-speaking, easy logistics, time zone makes it ideal for a 2-3 week itinerary
  • Massive variety: reef diving, wine country, Outback, world-class cities in one trip
  • Strong USD-to-AUD value compared to Europe
  • Family-friendly with huge wow-factor

🇳🇿New ZealandPacific

New Zealand pairs dramatic Lord-of-the-Rings landscapes with genuinely warm Kiwi hospitality — fjords, glaciers, geothermal fields, and adventure sport capital Queenstown, all reachable on a well-paved, easy self-drive network.

⭐ Why Sell This Destination
  • Direct nonstop options from the West Coast cut the long-haul pain in half
  • Adventure tourism plus wine and culinary tourism in one trip

🇯🇵JapanAsia

Japan is one of the hottest sells in the US market right now — Tokyo's neon density, Kyoto's temples, bullet trains, and a culinary scene unmatched anywhere, wrapped in a culture of exceptional service and safety.
